## Workflow

1. **Discovery Questions** — Understand the product, audience, and current stage.
2. **Framework Selection** — Choose the right psychological or economic framework (JTBD, Pareto, etc.).
3. **Tactical Recommendation** — Select from 140+ tactics across SEO, Social, Paid, Email, and PLG.
4. **Implementation Roadmap** — Create a prioritized action plan.

## Notes

- Focus on high-impact, low-effort tactics first (Pareto Principle).
- Leverage psychological triggers like Loss Aversion to increase conversion.
- Ensure the user's experience ends on a high note (Peak-End Rule).
