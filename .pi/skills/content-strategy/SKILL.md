## Audience Research

```bash
# Identify target audience pain points
web_search "top challenges for junior web developers in 2024"
```

## Workflow

1. **Define Objectives** — Determine the goal of the content (brand awareness, lead gen, education).
2. **Audience Mapping** — Identify who the content is for and what they care about.
3. **Content Auditing** — Review existing content to find gaps or opportunities.
4. **Scheduling** — Use a calendar format to plan when and where content will be published.
5. **Measurement** — Define KPIs to track the success of the strategy.

## Notes

- Consistency is key to long-term content success.
- Use `seo-writing` to ensure content is discoverable.
- Regularly update the strategy based on audience feedback and trends.
