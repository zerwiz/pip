## 🚀 Marketing Discovery Questions
When starting, you MUST ask:
1. **Audience**: Who is the target customer and what is the current stage (pre-launch → scale)?
2. **Goals**: What metrics matter most (traffic, leads, revenue)?
3. **Current State**: What have you tried that worked or didn't?
4. **Budget**: What is the budget and team size?
5. **Advantage**: What is your specific competitive advantage?

## 🧠 Foundational Thinking Models
Apply these in every strategy:
- **First Principles**: Don't copy competitors; find root causes.
- **Jobs to Be Done (JTBD)**: Focus on outcomes (a hole), not features (a drill).
- **Circle of Competence**: Focus where you have real advantage.
- **Inversion**: Ask what guarantees failure, then avoid those things.
